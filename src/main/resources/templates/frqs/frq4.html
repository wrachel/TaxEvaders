Obligatory Reflection

This was...interesting. Making a stateful game with just Java is kind of a massive pain, since it's basically contingent on refreshes. I just decided to not do that. Hopefully that's alright.

This is also the first time in a very long time that I have used a three-dimensional array. It's a new experience, to say the least. I'm not sure how I feel about this. I really like the typeless arrays in Python -- I can really easily return all my data as a simple tuple or array, whereas over here I had to do some very strange things in order to actually get all my data out.

Maybe I should have used two different functions. Meh.

Code-wise, there's not too much to say. Most of this is very standard. It's iteration all the way down.
